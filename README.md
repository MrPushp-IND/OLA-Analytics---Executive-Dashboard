## Overview

Ride-hailing is a thin-margin, high-volume business: small shifts in cancellations, peak-hour coverage, or payment mix move real money. This dashboard gives an operations leader a single view of the four levers that matter — **revenue, demand, drivers, and payments** — built in Power BI on a ride-level dataset.

## What the dashboard answers

- **Revenue** — total bookings value, revenue trend over time, and contribution by vehicle category
- **Demand** — ride volume by day and hour, identifying peak windows and slow periods worth incentivising
- **Drivers** — ratings distribution and performance patterns across the driver base
- **Payments** — method mix (cash vs UPI vs cards) and what it implies for settlement and reconciliation
- **Cancellations** — who cancels (customer vs driver), when, and the revenue left on the table
